Who We Are

Providing a Bright Start for Every Child

Astra Kidz is a premier preschool dedicated to fostering a love for learning in young minds. Through a carefully structured, play-based curriculum, our experienced educators guide children as they discover their potential, build friendships, and explore the wonders of the world around them.

We believe that every child is unique and deserves a supportive environment that builds self-confidence, curiosity, and emotional strength.

Safe Environment

CCTV monitored, fully secure, and child-proofed indoor & outdoor facilities.

Play-Based Learning

Activities designed to stimulate critical thinking and motor skills through active play.

Expert Teachers

Warm, certified educators trained in child psychology and early education care.

Creative Arts

Extensive opportunities for music, painting, dancing, and creative self-expression.

Our Classes

Programs Designed for Every Stage

Our age-appropriate learning programs focus on developing cognitive, emotional, and social capacities of children in their formative years.

1 - 3 Years

Toddlers Playgroup

Focuses on sensory play, early language development, fine motor skills, and building basic social habits in a loving environment.

3 Hours/Day 1:5 Ratio
3 - 4 Years

Preschool Explorers

Encourages creative arts, basic vocabulary, simple math concepts, shapes, storytelling, and early self-independence skills.

4 Hours/Day 1:8 Ratio
4 - 6 Years

Kindergarten Scholars

Prepares kids for primary school with phonics reading, elementary science concepts, logical reasoning, and cooperative group play.

5 Hours/Day 1:10 Ratio
